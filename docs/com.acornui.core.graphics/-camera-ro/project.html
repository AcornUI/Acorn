open fun project(globalCoords: Vector3, viewportX: Float, viewportY: Float, viewportWidth: Float, viewportHeight: Float): Vector3

Projects the {@link Vector3} given in global space to screen coordinates. The screen coordinate system has its origin in the top left, with the y-axis pointing downwards and the x-axis pointing to the right.

Parameters

viewportX - the coordinate of the top left corner of the viewport in glViewport coordinates.

viewportY - the coordinate of the top left corner of the viewport in glViewport coordinates.

viewportWidth - the width of the viewport in pixels

viewportHeight - the height of the viewport in pixels
