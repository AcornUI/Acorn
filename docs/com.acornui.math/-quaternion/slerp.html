fun slerp(q: Array<Quaternion>): Quaternion

Spherical linearly interpolates multiple quaternions and stores the result in this Quaternion. Will not destroy the data previously inside the elements of q. result = (q_1^w_1)(q2^w2) ... *(q_n^w_n) where w_i=1/n.

fun slerp(q: Array<Quaternion>, w: FloatArray): Quaternion

Spherical linearly interpolates multiple quaternions by the given weights and stores the result in this Quaternion. Will not destroy the data previously inside the elements of q or w. result = (q_1^w_1)(q2^w2) ... *(q_n^w_n) where the sum of w_i is 1. Lists must be equal in length.
